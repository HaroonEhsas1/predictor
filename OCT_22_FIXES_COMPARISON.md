## 🔧 FIXES THAT MADE THE DIFFERENCE

### Fix #1: Market Regime Detection
- **What it does**: Checks SPY/QQQ before making predictions
- **Oct 22 Impact**: Market was down -0.01% → Applied -0.05 bearish bias
- **Result**: Reduced bullish conviction across all stocks

### Fix #2: Technical Veto Power ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT
- **What it does**: When technical conflicts, reduce confidence and score
- **Oct 22 Impact**: ORCL's technical warning (-0.078) was CORRECT
- **Result**: Dropped ORCL confidence from 71% → 50% → FILTERED OUT

### Fix #3: Options Conflict Adjustment
- **What it does**: Reduce options weight when it conflicts with news+technical
- **Oct 22 Impact**: AVGO's options (-0.110) conflicted with news+tech (+0.126)
- **Result**: Reduced options influence by 50%

### Fix #4: Confidence Penalty for Conflicts
- **What it does**: Penalize confidence when multiple signals conflict
- **Oct 22 Impact**: AVGO had conflicting signals but 81% confidence
- **Result**: Reduced confidence to more realistic level

### Fix #5: News Freshness Decay
- **What it does**: Discount news that's >4 hours old
- **Oct 22 Impact**: AMD's news from morning was stale
- **Result**: Reduced news influence by 50%
